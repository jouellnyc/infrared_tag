## Introduction

Remember Laser tag? Put one of these on your desk and one on your co-worker and using the remote, it's shoot out until 3 shots hit...

## Components
- IR Receiver
- Pi Pico
- Leds - Red, Yellow, Green
- 470 Ohm (or similar) resistors
